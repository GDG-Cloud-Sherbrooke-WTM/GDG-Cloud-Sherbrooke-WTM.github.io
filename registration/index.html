The "Redefine Possible" theme encourages women in tech to question the current state of affairs and envision a future where technology is used to solve complex problems, create new opportunities, and expand what's achievable. This theme aims to empower women in tech to break barriers, become leaders, and inspire others to reimagine what's possible, especially in the face of rapid technological advancements like AI.

It's a call to explore the transformative power of AI and other technological advancements, leveraging their potential to shape a future that was once deemed impossible.
