Sakshi Gupta

Impacting the Future: 5 Steps to Get Started

Bio

Sakshi Gupta a pioneering force in the tech industry, renowned for her unwavering commitment to innovation and empowerment. As a Women Techmaker Ambassador, she has spent over a decade dedicated to advocating for diversity and inclusion in technology, inspiring countless individuals along the way. At the helm of the GDG Silicon Valley chapter, Sakshi has led with vision and passion, fostering a vibrant community of tech enthusiasts, developers, and innovators. As the CEO and founder of a Developer Marketing Consulting Company in Silicon Valley, she has demonstrated her expertise in technology and marketing. Her passion for fostering diversity and inclusion in tech has made her a sought-after speaker and mentor, inspiring others to pursue their ambitions in the field.

Summary

By breaking barriers, driving innovation, bridging the gender gap, empowering the next generation, and leveraging diversity for social impact, women in tech can make a significant difference. Let's continue to support and champion women in tech as we work towards a more inclusive and equitable future for all.
