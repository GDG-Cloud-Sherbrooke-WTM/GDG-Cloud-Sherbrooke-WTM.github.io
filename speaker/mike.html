Mike Hoff

7 Things that Movies can Teach you in Growing Your I.T. Career (And 1 that they can’t)

Bio

Mike has been in IT for over 30 years - yes, he's older than he looks! - including roles as Programmer, DBA, Sys Admin, BA, PMP, and various leadership positions. He's experienced the highs and lows of IT in retail, healthcare, government, consulting, a dot com, and a brief stint as IT Manager at Winsport Canada. Mike does occasional public speaking engagements on Job Hunting and Risk Management, and is famous for telling anyone who will listen that our role is to be "the midwives for The Cloud."

Summary

As an IT professional and movie critic, there are several key behaviours and traits to learn from movies and Hollywood in general.
